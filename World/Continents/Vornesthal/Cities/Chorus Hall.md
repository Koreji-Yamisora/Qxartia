# Chorus Hall
_Capital of the Stone-Singer Confederation_

> Part of [[Stone-Singer Confederation]] · [[Vornesthal]] · [[Qxartia]]

---

## Overview

Chorus Hall is built in the only place where all five tribal notes can be heard at once — a natural amphitheater where wind creates an ever-shifting symphony. The city is carved from the same pale stone as the [[Singing Pillars]], and no building is permitted to exceed their height.

---

## The Wind-Songs

The city's music changes with weather:
- **Calm days**: Gentle harmony; good for council meetings
- **Storm winds**: Dissonant clash; no decisions made
- **The Convergence**: Once yearly, all notes align perfectly — sacred moment

---

## Landmarks

- **The Five Columns**: Artificial pillars tuned to each tribe's note
- **The Chord-Keeper Archives**: Recorded harmonies for 2,000 years
- **The Listener's Platform**: Where the Listener-Prime interprets the Stone-Song
- **The Dissonance Chamber**: Where disputes are settled (soundproofed)
