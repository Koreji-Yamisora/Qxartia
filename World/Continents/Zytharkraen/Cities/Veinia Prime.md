# Veinia Prime
_Capital of the Veinian Oligarchy_

> Part of [[Veinian Oligarchy]] · [[Zytharkraen]] · [[Qxartia]]

---

## Overview

Veinia Prime is a city of stark contrasts — towering **Granary Spires** where the wealthy store [[Mana]]-preserved harvests rise above sprawling worker slums where families live season to season. The streets smell of grain dust, money, and quiet desperation.

---

## Districts

| District              | Description                                        |
| --------------------- | -------------------------------------------------- |
| **The Spire Quarter** | The Great Houses' estates and enchanted granaries  |
| **Market Spiral**     | Trade hub; prices change hourly                    |
| **The Tilling**       | Worker housing, taverns, labor exchanges           |
| **Goldsoil Row**      | Banking, loans, foreclosures                       |
| **The Outer Rot**     | Edge slums where [[Entropy]] effects are strongest |

---

## Landmarks

- **The Seven Spires**: Massive silos enchanted to slow Entropy; each belongs to a Great House
- **The Exchange**: Where Seed-marks are traded; most volatile market in Qxartia
- **House Goldsoil Tower**: The tallest building; where debt is recorded and called in
- **The Servant Gate**: Where those in Harvest Servitude are processed
