## Overview

While [[Mercenary Companies]] handle military operations, the **Adventurer Guilds** serve individuals and small groups: escorting merchants, clearing monster infestations, recovering lost artifacts, or simply surviving journeys too dangerous for ordinary travelers.

Each continent has its own guild system, adapted to local conditions.

---

## The Three Continental Guilds

### The Pulse-Runners Guild (Zytharkraen)
_"Timing is survival."_

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Headquarters** | [[Veinia Prime]] |
| **Philosophy** | Work with the land's rhythms, not against them |
| **Specialty** | Fast-decay goods transport, Pulse-timed missions |
| **Notable** | Only guild with [[Marsh Warden]] liaison officers |

The Pulse-Runners understand that in [[Zytharkraen]], everything is temporary. They specialize in missions that require precise timing: delivering goods before they decay, extracting people from entropy zones before memories fade, and coordinating with the [[Pulse-Keepers]]' schedules.

**Common Quests:**
- Harvest escort (high-value, short window)
- Marsh-edge reconnaissance
- Decay-zone recovery
- Night-caster tracking (CONTROVERSIAL)

### The Stone-Walkers Guild (Vornesthal)
_"Every echo guides us."_

| Aspect | Detail |
|--------|--------|
| **Headquarters** | [[Chorus Hall]] |
| **Philosophy** | Listen to the land; it tells you what it needs |
| **Specialty** | Underground navigation, cave operations, pillar-field work |
| **Notable** | Strong ties to [[Stone-Singer Confederation]] |

The Stone-Walkers are the preeminent adventurers of [[Vornesthal]] â€” skilled in navigating the [[Singing Pillars]], the [[Fungal Underdark]], and the dangerous canyon networks. They train members to navigate by sound and survive on minimal resources.

**Common Quests:**
- Caravan escort through pillar-fields
- Deep cave exploration
- Mana-fungi harvesting (dangerous variety)
- Lost expedition recovery
