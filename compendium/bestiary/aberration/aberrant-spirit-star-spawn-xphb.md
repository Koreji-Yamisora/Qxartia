## Traits

***Whispering Aura.*** At the start of each of the spirit's turns, the spirit emits psionic energy if it doesn't have the [Incapacitated](compendium/rules/conditions.md#Incapacitated) condition. *Wisdom Saving Throw:* DC equals your spell save DC, each creature (other than you) within 5 feet of the spirit. *Failure:* 2d6 Psychic damage.

## Actions

***Multiattack.*** The spirit makes a number of attacks equal to half this spell's level (round down).

***Psychic Slam.*** *Melee Attack Roll:* Bonus equals your spell attack modifier, reach 5 ft. *Hit:* 1d8 + 3 + the spell's level Psychic damage.
