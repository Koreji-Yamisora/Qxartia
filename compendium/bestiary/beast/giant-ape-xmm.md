## Actions

***Multiattack.*** The ape makes two Fist attacks.

***Fist.*** *Melee Attack Roll:* +9, reach 10 ft. *Hit:* 22 (3d10 + 6) Bludgeoning damage.

***Boulder Toss (Recharge 6).*** The ape hurls a boulder at a point it can see within 90 feet. *Dexterity Saving Throw:* DC 17, each creature in a 5-foot-radius [Sphere](compendium/rules/variant-rules/sphere-area-of-effect-xphb.md) centered on that point. *Failure:* 24 (7d6) Bludgeoning damage. If the target is a Large or smaller creature, it has the [Prone](compendium/rules/conditions.md#Prone) condition. *Success:* Half damage only.

## Bonus Actions

***Leap.*** The ape jumps up to 30 feet by spending 10 feet of movement.
