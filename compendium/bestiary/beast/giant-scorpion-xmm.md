***Multiattack.*** The scorpion makes two Claw attacks and one Sting attack.

***Claw.*** *Melee Attack Roll:* +5, reach 5 ft. *Hit:* 6 (1d6 + 3) Bludgeoning damage. If the target is a Large or smaller creature, it has the [Grappled](compendium/rules/conditions.md#Grappled) condition (escape DC 13) from one of two claws.
