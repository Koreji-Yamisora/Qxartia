## Traits

***Swarm.*** The swarm can occupy another creature's space and vice versa, and the swarm can move through any opening large enough for a Tiny bat. The swarm can't regain [Hit Points](compendium/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) or gain [Temporary Hit Points](compendium/rules/variant-rules/temporary-hit-points-xphb.md).

## Actions

***Bites.*** *Melee Attack Roll:* +4, reach 5 ft. *Hit:* 5 (2d4) Piercing damage, or 2 (1d4) Piercing damage if the swarm is [Bloodied](compendium/rules/conditions.md#Bloodied).
