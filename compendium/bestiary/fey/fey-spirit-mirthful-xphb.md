## Actions

***Multiattack.*** The spirit makes a number of Fey Blade attacks equal to half this spell's level (round down).

***Fey Blade.*** *Melee Attack Roll:* Bonus equals your spell attack modifier, reach 5 ft. *Hit:* 2d6 + 3 + the spell's level Force damage.

## Bonus Actions

***Fey Step.*** The spirit magically teleports up to 30 feet to an unoccupied space it can see. *Wisdom Saving Throw:* DC equals your spell save DC, one creature the spirit can see within 10 feet of itself. *Failure:* The target is [Charmed](compendium/rules/conditions.md#Charmed) by you and the spirit for 1 minute or until the target takes any damage.
