# Bracers of Archery
*Wondrous item, uncommon (requires attunement)*  


While wearing these bracers, you have proficiency with the [Longbow](compendium/items/longbow-xphb.md) and [Shortbow](compendium/items/shortbow-xphb.md), and you gain a +2 bonus to damage rolls made with such weapons.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 240. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
