# Ear Horn of Hearing
*Wondrous item, common*  


While held up to your ear, this horn suppresses the effects of the [Deafened](compendium/rules/conditions.md#Deafened) condition on you.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 256*
