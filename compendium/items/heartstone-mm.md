# Heartstone
*Very rare*  


This lustrous black gem allows a [night hag](compendium/bestiary/fiend/night-hag-xmm.md) to become ethereal while it is in her possession. The touch of a heartstone also cures any disease. Crafting a heartstone takes 30 days.

*Source: Monster Manual p. 179*
