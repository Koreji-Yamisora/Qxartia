# Potion of Superior Healing
*Potion, rare*  

- **Weight**: 0.5 lbs.

You regain `8d4 + 8` [Hit Points](compendium/rules/variant-rules/hit-points-xphb.md) when you drink this potion. The potion's red liquid glimmers when agitated.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 288. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
