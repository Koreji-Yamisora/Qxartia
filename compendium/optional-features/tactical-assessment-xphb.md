# Tactical Assessment
*Source: Player's Handbook (2024) p. 95*  

When you make an Intelligence ([History](compendium/rules/skills.md#History) or [Investigation](compendium/rules/skills.md#Investigation)) check or a Wisdom ([Insight](compendium/rules/skills.md#Insight)) check, you can expend one Superiority Die and add that die to the ability check.
