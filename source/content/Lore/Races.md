## Overview

The peoples of [[Qxartia]] are diverse, each with origins tied to the planes of existence. Some trace their lineage to the [[Material Plane]] alone, while others bear the marks of [[The Feywild]], [[The Shadowfell]], [[The Elemental Chaos]], or even [[The Celestial]] before [[The Severing]].

---

## Common Races

### Humans
_"The Adaptable Ones"_

The most numerous race in Qxartia. Humans have no inherent planar connection ‚Äî they are purely of the Material Plane. This makes them both unremarkable and remarkably adaptable.

| Trait | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Origin** | Material Plane |
| **Lifespan** | 70-90 years |
| **Traits** | Versatile, ambitious, short-lived but prolific |
| **Continent Presence** | Dominant in all three continents |

> üí° *Humans often rise to power precisely because they have no magical heritage to rely on ‚Äî they must earn everything.*

---

### Elves
_"The Fey-Touched"_

Elves carry the blood of [[The Feywild]] in their veins. Long ago, before [[The Severing]], elves crossed freely between the Material Plane and the Feywild. Now, that path is more difficult, but the connection remains.

| Subrace | Origin | Description |
|---------|--------|-------------|
| **High Elves** | Ancient Feywild settlers | Arcane magic, long memory, slightly arrogant |
| **Wood Elves** | Material Plane adapted | Swift, stealthy, deeply tied to forests |
| **Eladrin** | Pure Feywild natives | Emotions shift with seasons; rare visitors |
| **Shadar-kai** | [[The Shadowfell]] adapted | Gray-skinned, fierce, serve mysterious powers |
| **Drow** | Underdark dwellers | Dark-skinned, matriarchal, complex politics |

| Trait | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Origin** | Feywild / Shadowfell |
| **Lifespan** | 700+ years |
| **Traits** | Keen senses, trance meditation, resistance to charm |

---

### Dwarves
_"The Stone-Singers"_

Dwarves claim they were shaped by the primordials of [[The Elemental Chaos]] ‚Äî specifically the earth primordial Grumbar. Whether true or Imperial propaganda, dwarves have an undeniable connection to stone.

| Subrace | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Hill Dwarves** | Hardy, wise, close to the surface |
| **Mountain Dwarves** | Strong, martial, deep delvers |
| **Duergar** | Shadowfell-touched, gray-skinned, psionic |

| Trait | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Origin** | Elemental Chaos (earth) |
| **Lifespan** | 350+ years |
| **Traits** | Darkvision, poison resistance, stonecunning |
| **Continent Presence** | Strongest in [[Vornesthal]] |

---

### Halflings
_"The Lucky Folk"_

Halflings are entirely of the Material Plane ‚Äî perhaps the most purely mortal of all races. They have no planar heritage, which some scholars believe makes them "invisible" to cosmic forces.

| Subrace | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Lightfoot** | Stealthy, social, wanderers |
| **Stout** | Hardy, poison-resistant, homebodies |

| Trait | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Origin** | Material Plane |
| **Lifespan** | 150 years |
| **Traits** | Lucky, brave, nimble |
| **Continent Presence** | Scattered everywhere, rarely in power |

---

### Orcs
_"The Fierce Ones"_

Orcs are a proud warrior race, often at odds with other civilizations. Their origins are disputed ‚Äî some say they were created by a war-god of [[The Pantheon]], others that they evolved naturally in the harshest regions.

| Trait | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Origin** | Material Plane (possibly divine creation) |
| **Lifespan** | 50-60 years |
| **Traits** | Powerful build, aggressive, tribal society |
| **Continent Presence** | Strongest in [[Zytharkraen]] borderlands; clans scattered elsewhere |

> ‚ö†Ô∏è *The [[Fallen Holy Empire]] classified orcs as "monstrous" rather than a race, a prejudice that persists in some regions.*

---

### Gnomes
_"The Tinkerers"_

Gnomes are cousins to the fey, though more distant than elves. Their connection to [[The Feywild]] manifests as an affinity for illusion magic and an insatiable curiosity.

| Subrace | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Rock Gnomes** | Inventors, artificers, gadget-makers |
| **Forest Gnomes** | Illusionists, animal speakers, secretive |
| **Deep Gnomes (Svirfneblin)** | Underdark dwellers, stealthy, gemcutters |

| Trait | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Origin** | Feywild (distant) |
| **Lifespan** | 400+ years |
| **Traits** | Darkvision, gnome cunning (advantage vs magic) |

## Uncommon Races

### Tieflings
_"The Infernal-Touched"_

Tieflings carry the blood of fiends ‚Äî demons from [[The Abyss]] or devils from the Nine Hells (an [[astral dominion]] that may still exist, though unconfirmed since [[The Severing]]). Their appearance marks them as outsiders.

| Trait | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Origin** | Abyss / Nine Hells influence |
| **Appearance** | Horns, tails, unusual skin colors |
| **Traits** | Darkvision, fire resistance, innate magic |
| **Social Status** | Feared, distrusted, often persecuted |

---

### Aasimar
_"The Celestial-Touched"_

Aasimar carry traces of celestial blood ‚Äî from before [[The Severing]], when [[The Celestial]] was still connected to the mortal world. They are rare now, and their births are considered omens.

| Subrace | Description |
|---------|-------------|
| **Protector** | Guardian spirits, healing light |
| **Scourge** | Burning radiance, righteous fury |
| **Fallen** | Touched by darkness, inner conflict |

| Trait | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Origin** | The Celestial (pre-Severing) |
| **Traits** | Darkvision, celestial resistance, healing hands |
| **Rarity** | Extremely rare since The Severing |

---

### Dragonborn
_"The Dragon-Blooded"_

Dragonborn claim descent from dragons ‚Äî creatures of both the Material Plane and [[The Elemental Chaos]]. Their origins are disputed; some say they were created by dragons, others by primordials.

| Trait | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Origin** | Dragons / Elemental Chaos |
| **Traits** | Breath weapon, damage resistance (by ancestry) |
| **Continent Presence** | Rare everywhere; small enclaves |

## The Fallen Holy Empire and Race

The [[Fallen Holy Empire]] taught that:
- **Humans** are the chosen race of [[Selamonius|God]]
- **Fey-touched races** (elves, gnomes) are "corrupted" by wild magic
- **Infernal-touched** (tieflings) are damned from birth
- **Celestial-touched** (aasimar) are holy ‚Äî but suspiciously rare

This doctrine persists in some regions, creating tension between races.
