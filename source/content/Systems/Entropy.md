## Regional Manifestations

Entropy concentrates in **pockets**, not borders. The same continent can hold regions untouched for centuries beside places where everything forgets within hours.

### Temporal Entropy Zones
- Ruins where a day passes like a week
- Crossroads where travelers arrive before they left
- Gardens where flowers bloom and die in a single breath

### Material Entropy Zones
- Stone walls that crumble into sand overnight
- Rivers that dissolve metal but leave wood untouched
- Roads that must be rebuilt every season

### Memory Entropy Zones
- Villages where elders forget their grandchildren's names
- Libraries where ink fades from the page as you read
- Battlefields where the dead forget they've fallen

### Vital Entropy Zones
- Forests where trees grow tall then collapse into rot
- Farms where crops sprout gray and tasteless
- Ponds where fish swim slower each day until they stop

### Arcane Entropy Zones
- Ritual sites where spells unravel mid-casting
- [[Mana]] wells that drain faster than they fill
- Enchanted objects that lose their glow within hours

### Sensory Entropy Zones
- Caves where echoes arrive before the sound
- Mists where north feels like south
- Twilight groves where colors bleed into gray
