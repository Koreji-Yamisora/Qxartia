# Breathlight
_Holy Capital of the Pulse-Keepers_

> Part of [[Pulse-Keepers]] · [[Zytharkraen]] · [[Qxartia]]

---

## Overview

Breathlight is a city of white stone and profound silence — until dawn. Built atop a nexus where [[the Pulse]] is strongest, the entire city is designed around the moment when the land exhales [[Mana]]. Every tower, every street, every window is positioned to catch the first light.

---

## Districts

| District | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| **The Dawn Steps** | Central plaza; fills with worshippers each sunrise |
| **Temple Heights** | Religious buildings, clergy quarters |
| **The Timing Markets** | Where casting schedules are consulted and sold |
| **Scholar's Row** | Pulse-Scribes and researchers |
| **The Shadow District** | Where night-casters allegedly gather |

---

## The Morning Hymn

Every day at dawn, bells ring from every temple. The entire city sings — a hymn thanking the Pulse for another breath. Those who don't sing are noticed.

---

## Landmarks

- **The Voice's Temple**: Where the Voice of Dawn resides; tallest spire
- **The Sundial Plaza**: Massive central circle; shadows mark sacred moments
- **The Archives of Exhalation**: Records of every Pulse for 3,000 years
- **The Night Courts**: Underground; officially "re-education centers"
